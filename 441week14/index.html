About Rise Academy

Rise Academy is a leading provider of accredited vocational education and training in Australia. Located at 456 Education Boulevard, Melbourne VIC 3000, we are dedicated to delivering high-quality courses that meet industry standards and empower students with practical skills for their careers. Our mission is to bridge the gap between education and employment through flexible learning options and expert-led training.

Our Unique Values

  • Accredited Excellence: All our courses are nationally recognized and delivered by Registered Training Organizations (RTOs) listed on training.gov.au, ensuring compliance with Australian standards.
  • Industry-Relevant Training: We collaborate with industry experts to design courses that align with current market demands, equipping students with job-ready skills.
  • Flexible Learning: Choose from online, in-person, or hybrid study modes to fit your schedule and learning preferences.
